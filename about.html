About

The main aim of this project is to provide the local people such as the unemployed,
daily wage labourers, part time workers,students, interns, co -workers and people who
somehow lost their job amidst this pandemic to get a new kickstart towards their specific
work life.

This project works irrespective of people's standardized jobs which not only provides job/work
seeking opportunities to the educated as well as the local skilled operators and labourers to
create an profile of their own and find suitable work/jobs by profile matching through the JobLoc
portal.

This project aims to provide better and fast job recommendation to the people with precise matching
of the profile of user and the job provider. Not only the depending on profile matching but the users
can also get job vacancies as per requirement from the online website. Users have to register for login
and then fill their personal information, qualification details, skills, past work details. If entire
details are filled properly then based on vacancies the resume of user will be generated. Based on the
profiles of the users and company matching , a job is recommended to user. We can also provide the android app which helps in faster notification to users about vacancies.
